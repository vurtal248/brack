## Design Context

### Users
Tournament organizers, gamers, and friends running local competitions. They need a fast, precise, and completely reliable interface that doesn't get in their way and works completely client-side.

### Brand Personality
Technical, precise, solid, utilitarian.

### Aesthetic Direction
Neo-Brutalism meets Swiss Grid. High-contrast borders, monochromatic text, clear bounding boxes. No unnecessary gradients, no floating shapes. Function defines form.

### Design Principles
1. **Data over Decoration**: Heavy borders, clear tabular layouts, utilitarian styling.
2. **Absolute Clarity**: Interactive states (focus, hover, win, lose) must be undeniable and bold. 
3. **Snappy Motion**: Use sharp exponential deceleration (ease-out-quint) rather than slow fluid moves. Interfaces should feel instant and mechanical.
